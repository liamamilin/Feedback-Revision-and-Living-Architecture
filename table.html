2  How Structures Remain Open, Adaptive, and Real

Volume VIII — Feedback, Revision, and Living Architecture

2.1 Volume Mission

To explain how structures remain alive by receiving feedback, testing against reality, updating models, revising boundaries, and adapting over time.

中文：

解释结构如何通过接收反馈、接受现实检验、更新模型、修正边界和长期适应而保持活性。

2.2 Core Question

How does a structure remain open to reality instead of becoming a dead framework or ideology?

中文：

结构如何保持对现实开放，而不是变成死框架或意识形态？

2.3 Scope

This volume studies feedback, case testing, reality contact, failure signal, model update, boundary revision, scope revision, concept drift, structure drift, iteration, living architecture, anti-dogmatism, practice feedback, and long-term refinement.
